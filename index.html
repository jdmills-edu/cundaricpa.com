ABOUT CUNDARI & CUNDARI

We are committed to our clients' needs.

Background

We are a husband and wife team, having both worked in public accounting as well as industry. Our practice had its origin in New Jersey and we retained the majority of our accounts when we moved to South Carolina 10 years ago. We are both licensed CPAs in New Jersey and South Carolina and can file returns in any state. Affiliations include memberships with the American Institute of CPAs and State CPA Society.
